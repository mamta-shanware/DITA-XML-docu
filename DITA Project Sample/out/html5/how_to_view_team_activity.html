How to view team activity

Here you will get instructions about how to view team activity.

To successfully view team activity:
  • You must be logged into the application.
  • Have access rights to view dashboards and project data.
  1. Click Dashboard > My Dashboard.
  2. Use default widgets(e.g. "Task due today" , " Overdue tasks").
  3. Filter by user or project.

During morning standup, you check:

  • What's overdue
  • Tasks completed by each team
