What is the Division of numbers?

Here you will learn what is Division of numbers.

To successfully perform the task of dividing numbers, you must understand what division is. The term “Division” represents a mathematical operation used to split a number into equal parts. Division is a process you perform using numbers. It lets you find out how many times one number is contained within another. In the App, you must use the divide (÷) button to perform the division operation. In this process, we take a larger number and break it down into smaller, equal groups by dividing it by another number.
