How to multiply numbers

Here you will find instructions about using the Multiplication feature of the JPS Calculator.

To successfully multiply numbers, you must know what is multiplication or product of numbers.
  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the multiply (x) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equal (=) button.

The result from the multiplication is displayed.

You want to multiply 5 with 3. The equation will look like 5x3=15. For this, first you enter 5. Press multiply button. Then you enter 3. Now press the equals button. The result 15 appears on the screen.
