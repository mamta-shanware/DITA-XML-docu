How to invite users

Here you will get instructions about how to invite users.

To successfully invite users
  • You must be logged into the application.
  • Have permission to invite new members to the workspace or project.
  1. Go to Settings > Members.
  2. Click Invite Member.
  3. Enter email and choose a role: Admin, Contributor, Viewer.
  4. Send invite.
